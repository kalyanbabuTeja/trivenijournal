JUDAS

 

SADANANDA REGE

(Translated from the original in Marathi by

DR PRABHAKAR MACHWE)

 

            On the branches or darkness

            Blades of lightning

            Around the ramparts of flash tempest

            Nights of deception clash

            In crushed dead leaves

            Drops of uncertainty drip

            Under the emptiness of sky

            Bats of malice sleep

            On a branch of a tree

            A rope

                        dangles

            In the noose of the rope

            A corpse

                        swings

            Around the corpse

            Dreadful wind

                        sings

            And in his pocket

            Thirty coins

                        jingle.
