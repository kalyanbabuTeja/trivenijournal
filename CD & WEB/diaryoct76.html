DIARY

 

BIRESHWAR BARUA

(Translated from Assamese by P. MACHWE)

 

That was a day when all the sadness of the world was collected in the sky

the boatmen had hid their faces in their knees

but boats were floating ogling their homes in the water

and the dry rocks of Hiroshima covered them.

 

From many days the sky is clear

yesterday night the moon was rising on the house of a fisherman

I had seen the mist and the smoke and dimness

wild flowers in every path

the face of Anuradha dream-lost in a distant village.
