FAREWELL

 

Endless is life,

Full of useless strife.

A fleeting moment,

A twinkle,

A star,

A shiny bubble,

A joyous event,

Such is love.

 

We meet,

Our bond incomplete,

We depart,

In vain search of art.

Memory persists,

And life exists.

 

To far-off lands you go,

Seeking fame,

Don’t you know–  

You kindled an ever burning flame?

 

I thought all night,

Your image glaring at me,

Silent now at your flight,

May I say–

Adieu, Chere Amie.

–TORU
