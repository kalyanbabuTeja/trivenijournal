GREETINGS TO ‘TRIVENI’

 

Dear Mr. Ramakotiswara Rao,

I greatly appreciate your self-sacrificing labours for conducting your periodical, ‘TRIVENI’. It is, what you call it, really a journal of Indian renaissance. It is a fine specimen of our cultural enterprise. I hope you will be able to convert it into a monthly in another year or two.

With kind regards and best wishes,

Yours sincerely,

RAMANANDA CHATTERJEE,

Editor, "The Modern Review"
