THE EMPTY BOWL

 

My merits and my sins

My sorrows and my joys

My thoughts and my deeds

My earnings all,

Were stored since my birth

In the bowl of my heart.

 

I have now outpoured that bowl,

And

I have taken Refuge in the Buddha

I have taken Refuge in the Dhamma

I have taken Refuge in the Sangha.

 

O Tathagata!

The emptied bowl I hold,

Now I stand before you;

O Serene

O Free!

That you may fill it to the brim

With the Nectar of your Bodhi– 

The Sublime,

The Eternal!

 

–SUJATA PRIYAMBADA

Translated from Oriya By SHANTIBHADRA
