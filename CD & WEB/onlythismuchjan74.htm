ONLY THIS MUCH

 

MANGESH PADGAONKAR

(Translated from Marathi by DR PRABHAKAR MACHWE)

 

In those hair of wavy nights

dim weak fragrance

five finger-birds moving

had lost themselves...

 

and unconsciously

again

they returned

with five shy stars

in their beaks

 

you took your night away

but yet

fingers whisperingly sing

only this much

is left for myself.
