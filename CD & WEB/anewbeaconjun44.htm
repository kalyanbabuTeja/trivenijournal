A New Beacon

 

BY K. K. KAUL

 

Through darkened world possessed by might-mare

Comes sweeping a hurricane of fire;

            Nations vulture-like

            Hover shamelessly

            Where stifled anguish bleeds.

            Whirlwind of killing goes on.

            Hungry flames mount ever higher,

            In unending mathematics.

           

Out of a crimson tempest,

A red volcanic sky

Hell unpent

A new beacon shines on the horizon of future, storms.
