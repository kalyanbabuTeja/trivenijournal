THE LIGHT OF SWAMI VIVEKANANDA

 

Hail thee Swami Vivekananda

Who brought us the glory of Vivek and Ananda

And broke the whispering chord of evil sentiments

That in every human birth ascends.

 

Hail thee saint, thou was born to rule this earth

To reign supreme forever from thy birth

We hail thee, worshipper of thine own mother and peace

Whose echo dwells that tyranny forever must cease.

 

Thy verse exuberant lavishly thrown for men

To save themselves from their earthly den

Thy name, thy words, thy glory forever shall be

Thy sacred hymns this worldly man shall see

And rise to love, love thy god alone

And reach him where he lives, wherever his light has shone.

 

–R. GOPIKRISHNAN NAIR
