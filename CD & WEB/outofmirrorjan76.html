OUT OF MIRROR

 

Forgotten days repeat

when pebbles strike hard

injured limbs mature

awaiting perfection

while ‘injury’ as bad name

and ‘limbs’ as courageous

 

not a story of anatomy

 

my absent-mindedness

affect my faults

even others’ deceptions

to me forgotten

 

a heightened glory

 

the next sunrise

discovers me pierced

and to compose my eyes smile.

–NARU MOHANTY
