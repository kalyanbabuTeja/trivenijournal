BLISSFUL DAWN

 

From the womb

of the dark night

Struggles out

The twittering dawn

With its twilighty tranquil

And with its divine dewy wetness

To dispel the viel of gloom

By its serene, scintilating touch,

With its lilting, refreshing breezy hum,

From the sick and dispirited

Face of sulky nature

And thus spreads

The twilighty viel of bliss

on the anxious face of dull nature,

And immerses it into

The sprightly abyss of dizzy rapture...

Looking at this twittering dawn

My dingy soul echoes:

When that blissful dawn

Will twitter and chime

In my sterile life of sensuous slumber...

And when that sprightly and twilighty

Dawn of my spiritual awakening

Will struggle out

From the whirly womb

Of my dismal life,

And send my dead spirit

Into the dizzy heights of spiritual bliss.

–SYED AMEERUDDIN
