PERMANENCE

 

            Life is Short,

            Art is long,

            The bards of ages,

            the eternal truth sing.

 

            Yet in short span,

            the darlings of art,

            Beauty and truth scan,

            Speak universal values,

            Oozing at every pore.

 

            Why complain of shortness of life,

            One can find beauty in small measures,

            Creation knows no bounds of time,

            Nor fades in evanescent existence.

–N. V. RAMDAS, Orissa
