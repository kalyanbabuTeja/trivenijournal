Existence Beyond

 

By NANCHINNA

 

In the apparent stillness of this universe

There is the loudest song.

 

Each unit of matter we see around us

Is an integration.

Of the original spirit in part.

 

Too much brilliance makes us blind

And want of brilliance too;

The Infinite evades our senses,

The Infinitesimal too.

 

Whether beyond our maximum

Or beneath our minimum

Its both negation to us.
