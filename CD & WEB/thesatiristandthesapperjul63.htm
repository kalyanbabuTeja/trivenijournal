THE SATIRIST AND THE SAPPER

 

SERGEI MIKHAILOV

Translated from Russian

 

G. K. G. JOSHI

 

The Satirist is like the Sapper, friends,

He mines-and mine I!

The Sapper’s task is to see if there is root

To dig underneath the foe,

To remain undamaged oneself:

But if you have a trembling feel in hands

Quit the job in Sapper troops

Indeed, there are other posts in the world

Well, we will tell, to write in the reward-lists?

Here they follow, also having skill

So that the list can appear without blots!

–From Izvestia
