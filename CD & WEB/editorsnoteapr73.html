IN DEDICATING

 

This special number to the memory of Acharya K. SAMPATHGIRI RAO Former Associate Editor of “Triveni” – a true disciple of Mahatma Gandhi, a reputed educationist and a great linguist, a man of simple living and high thinking, a true scholar and a sturdy patriot, and a reliable friend and a hospitable host – I offer my grateful and respectful Pranams.

 

–Bhavaraju Narasimha Rao
