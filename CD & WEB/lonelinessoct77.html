LONELINESS

 

MISS KRISHNA BOSE, M. A.

 

I walked down

the soot-laden nauseating corridors

infested by worms, stinging wasps

blood-sucking leeches

tangled cobwebs.

Suddenly I discovered a white spot

unmapped like a patch of sky,

emitting white lights.

It blinded me, clasped me with human hands,

I saw thee frozen face of loneliness.
