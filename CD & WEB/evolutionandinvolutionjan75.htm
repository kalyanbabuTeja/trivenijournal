EVOLUTION AND INVOLUTION

 

Evolution and involution

Are not universal.

Molecules, atoms, atomic particles,

Ions, neutrons and photons,

Have been most zealously

Preserving their properties

Ever since the birth of the universe.

Bodies–living or non-living–

Are subjected to evolution and involution.

Mountains become boulders

And are exposed to

Chilling cold,

Devastating heat,

Cyclonic winds,

Torrential rains,

They break and break

Into particles of sand

And spread into arid deserts.

Volcanic eruptions

Create mountains.

Life Is subjugated to Time;

As the wheel of time revolves,

Unable to extricate itself

From its clamping clutches

Life passes thro’ the cycles

Of birth and death

Till it is finally emancipated.

 

–VASANTHARAO VENKATA RAO
