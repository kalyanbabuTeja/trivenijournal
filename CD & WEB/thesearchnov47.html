The Search

 

BY K. SESHADRI IYENGAR

 

Like the bee roaming from

Flower to flower seeking honey,

All Life is a search for Joy in little measures.

 

Unknown to ourselves,

Is it Him we are seeking–

The Great Source whence all Joys spring–

 

And only in that Great Meeting

Life may fulfillment find,

And the Journey come to an end?
