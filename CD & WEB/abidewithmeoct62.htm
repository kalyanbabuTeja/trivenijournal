ABIDE WITH ME

 

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me,

Only thy presence I yearn for,

World’s din and bustle binds me not–nor

Its riches, I long to step at thy side free!

Friend, a soul’s friend, abide with me.

 

Friend, friend, abide with me,

Surround with thy bliss that bends down the far sky,

Would that into thy sunny spheres I could fly,

Lay thy touch that makes a drop span infinity,

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me.

 

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me,

Let the strings be all astir with thy song,

Myriad notes of heaven’s lustre throng,

With thy magic flute ring my soul’s profundity,

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me.

 

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me,

Set aglow the dark, break the seal of sleep,

Reveal the dawn-white dreams hidden in the deep,

My one and all mate on the way of eternal journey,

Friend, O soul’s friend, abide with me.

 

–ROBI GUPTA
