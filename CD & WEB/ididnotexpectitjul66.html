I DID NOT EXPECT IT

 

If I merit

Your visit

For a moment only,

Then don’t make it.

 

My heart

Flares with a start

When you come

To the door,

Open wide.

 

My heart

Glares with anger

Plus desire

If you figure

At the door

And instantly bolt it– 

Because I did not expect it.

 

SUBHAS CHANDRA SAHA
