LIFE

 

By D. Y. RAMA RAO

 

My life has been a long winding lane.

 

Many a strange sight it has come across in the dark silent corners through which it passed.

 

Now that it has reached this bright high road, it has lost, alas, much of its old romance.

                                               

Whoever has known the mystery of this endless ocean, they call life?

 

I, who have never been a diver, know not what strange things may lurk in the dark fathomless depths.

 

I have ever been a glider, skating on the surface; yet, have I crossed and recrossed this strange ocean, while, alas! mighty swimmers and daring divers have come to grief in crossing this same strange ocean.
