It was Dusk

 

By VED PRAKASH SARIN

                                   

It was dusk.

                                    Strange enough

                                    In a dusky street

                                    A dog

                                    Ribbed

                                    Waited outside–

                                    Near a gutter–

                                    A butcher’s shop.

                                    The butcher burnt an earthen lamp

                                    With a steady glow,

                                    With a steady flame.    

                                    is Life waited on Death

                                    of And Death lit life.
