UNPREDICTABLE

 

D. V. KRISHNA. SASTRI

(Rendered from the Telugu original by D. ANJANEYULU)

 

Had I known before, Lord!

would I have kept this abode thus?

How stupid of me! Had I known

That sweet moment of your arrival, Lord 

 

A pretty carpet of

coral and jasmine petals

would have made

a feast for your eyes.

 

You wouldn’t come

After a long life’s wait

When least expected you are here

and gone away in a trice

Catching me unawares!

 

To keep you away here for ever

I can’t shackle your feet with my heart.
