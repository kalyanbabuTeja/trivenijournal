I see your face

 

BY ADI K. SETT

 

I see your face in the sky, suffused with gay colours at eventide,

I see your face in the glitter of lights at night,

I see your face in the dim gleam of autumn leaves,

I see your face in the transient colours of the seven seas.

I see your face in the snows on mountain peaks,

I see your face in the lush-gold corn in the fields.

I see your face in burnished halls of alabaster and porphyry,

I see your face in the glow of an anointed sanctuary.
