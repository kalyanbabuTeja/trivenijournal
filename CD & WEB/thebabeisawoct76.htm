THE BABE I SAW

 

SUJATA PRIYAMBADA

(Translated from Oriya by SHANTIBHADRA)

 

The babe I saw:

August, serene and boldly unconcerned;

And, as on a richly caprisoned throne

The babe reclining on the mother’s breast I saw.

 

I rubbed my eyes,

And mused and mused:

A prince, a king or an emperor

Or a little Gandhi, a Jawahar, a Lal Bahadur

Or the Hero of the heroes, Nataji–

Whichever I saw!
