My Love

BY DEVA-KULATTAR

(Translated by P. N. Appuswami from the original poem in Tamil)

[This confession of the nature of her love is made by a mistress to her maid.]

Wider than the earth,

Higher than the heavens,

And deeper far than the unplumbed sea

Is my love for him,

On whose hill-slopes

Bees make rich honey

From the flowers of the dark-stemmed henna.
