DARK NIGHT

 

D. V. KRISHNA SASTRI

 

            Call me not home!

            Nor ask me in

            Call me not home

            a lonely traveller by night

            along four street.

            Call me not home!

 

            Playing an unknown song

            on a modest lute,

            Picking up some dreams

            all along the path,

            Call me not home!

 

            With the door ajar, why nod your head

            As if to beckon me in?

            Shower not your affection unbearable

            To hold me up!

            I can’t stand, for I must sing

            The morning song before the Lord,

            At the break of dawn

            that follows the dark night.

            Call me not home!

[Rendered from the Telugu original by D. Anjaneyulu]
