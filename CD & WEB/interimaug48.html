INTERIM

 

By M. S. Gopalakrishnan, M.A.

 

BETWEEN the womb

and the grave,

between the pains

of delivery,

and the agonies

of death,

why creation struggles?

What is not what?

 

Between the embrace

and the tears,

between the fire

and the ashes,

between the sexes

and the sexless,

why creation struggles?

What is not what?
