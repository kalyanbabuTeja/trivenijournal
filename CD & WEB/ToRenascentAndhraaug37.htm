‘To Renascent Andhra’

BY PROF. RAYAPROLU SUBBA RAO 1

(Translated from Telugu by K. L. R.)

Look, the paths of heaven are free from clouds; our hopes rise forth to regions beyond ken; serene are the waters of the stream of Life; a new effulgence radiates the earth; and the garden has blossomed into flowers of new aspirations.

Behind the dark veil of the night, beyond the dewy curtains, far away, there shines a form divine, the quintessence of Light.

And your sisters have started before you on the journey.

Come, dear child!

 

1 From Tenugu Thota, a book of poems published in 1914.
