GREETINGS TO ‘TRIVENI’

By The Rt. Hon. Dr. V. S. Srinivasa Sastri

Dear Mr. Ramakotiswara Rao,

You have asked me often but I have not appeared once in your luminous pages. I have missed an honour, for the Triveni is a high-class production–bright and elegant. Its appeal is to a highly cultivated select few. To make it succeed is a difficult task. The men and women among us who combine taste, judgment, leisure and means are not many. I am inclined to congratulate you on the success you have attained. . . . I wish somehow writers and subscribers would alike make it possible for you to keep up the beautiful Triveni.

Sincerely yours,

V. S. Srinivasa Sastri

Coimbatore,

9th Sep. I933.
