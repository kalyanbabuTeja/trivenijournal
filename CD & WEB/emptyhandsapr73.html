EMPTY HANDS

 

TRILOCHAN

 

Departing you said: Bring this, bring that

I came back with both the hands empty

The bazaar was full of things, but what could I have

Without money. You know, I was not happy.

 

As here, so there, shall I say this?

This will hurt her? What shall she think

She had such expectations? Shall I tell

There are such people born in this world.

 

For whom a dime is also not available

They also receive love. The noise of happiness

Is heard by them too. Only misery

Meets them at every corner. No redress.

What should I give? What? What? What?

Where, what is within my reach which I may bring.

 

Translated from the original in Hindi by

DR. PRABHAKAR MACHWE
